My personal interests

I love motorcycles. I have a huge enthusiam for riding motorcycles. I ride a Harley Davidson which is currently my commute to work and University. I own a 2019 Iron 883 Sportster, the reason I chose this bike is because of the cc (cubic centimeters), this bike fits perfectly for me to give me enough power to ride fast and cruise yet not give me the ability to hurt myself. However, I would love to own a Dyna which is the fastest motorcycle Harley Davidson manufactures.

Another personal interest I enjoy is playing video games. I've grown up to enjoy video games to the point where played competitively. However, that messed with my mental health so now I enjoy playing video games casually and for fun which should've been the intent in the first place. Some games I really enjoy are League of Legends, Overwatch, Garry's Mod, DayZ. As you can see, they are all competitive but I try to play for fun mostly.

I've always been interested in sail boats, which is weird coming from a person who rides motorcycles and wants to be a software engineer as a career. The thought of sailing out on sea gives a sense of freedom and adventure which my soul loves to chase. I love the ocean and the thought of floating on God's creation. I also love the thought of island hopping, free diving, and enjoying the sun. One crazy fact is we've only discovered 5% of the ocean, crazy huh?
